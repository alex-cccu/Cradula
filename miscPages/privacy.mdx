# Privacy Policy

This page outlines the privacy practices of our application. We are committed to
protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency about how we
collect, use, and share your data.

---

Not to fear, you're in luck!

At this time we do not collect any personal information from users. We do not
use cookies, tracking technologies, or third-party services that collect data.

We may update this policy in the future however to reflect any changes in our
practices or legal requirements, so please check back here regularly to make
sure you are up to scratch with how we operate.

Even if we do update this page to help us with site telemetry, we will always
prioritise your privacy and always strive to keep any data we collect
anonymised, secure and minimal. We will never sell your data to third parties.
